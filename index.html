Henry Minh-Man Vo

I am a second year undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science at California State University, Sacramento (CSUS). In my leisure, I enjoy playing the flute and violin, playing video games, and working out.

Currently, I am acting as the treasurer for the Vietnamese Student Association at CSUS and a dance captain for its corresponding dance team, Red Lotus.

Formerly, I played the flute and piccolo for the Sacramento State Marching Band during Fall 2023, as well as the Monterey Trail High School Symphonic Ensemble and Marching Band during the 2019-2023 seasons.
